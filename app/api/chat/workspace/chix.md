# Chicken Rice Recipe

## Ingredients
- 2 chicken breasts, diced
- 1 cup long-grain rice
- 2 cups chicken broth
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 cup mixed vegetables
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Heat olive oil in a pan. Saut√© onion and garlic until fragrant.
2. Add diced chicken and cook until golden brown.
3. Stir in rice and cook for 2 minutes, stirring constantly.
4. Pour in chicken broth and bring to a boil.
5. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes.
6. Add mixed vegetables and cook for 5 more minutes.
7. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot.

**Serves:** 4 | **Time:** 30 minutes
